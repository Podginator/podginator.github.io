ABOUT MANCHESTER

____________________________________________________________________________________

Manchester is a city and metropolitan borough in North West England with an estimated population of 503,000.[4] Manchester lies within the United Kingdom's third largest urban area which has a population of 2,240,230.[5] People from Manchester are known as Mancunians and the local authority is Manchester City Council. Manchester is situated in the south-central part of North West England, fringed by the Cheshire Plain to the south and the Pennines to the north and east. Inhabitants of Manchester are referred to as Mancuniansor colloquially as Mancs.
